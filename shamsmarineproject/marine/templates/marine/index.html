Our Mission

At Shams Marine, we understand the vital role that ships and vessels play in global trade, transportation, and security. Our mission is to provide comprehensive and environmentally responsible ship maintenance solutions that enhance the longevity, safety, and performance of maritime assets.

Expertise and Experience

Our team of dedicated professionals brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to the table. We specialize in the maintenance of all types of vessels, from cargo ships and container carriers to offshore platforms and cruise liners. With an unwavering commitment to quality and safety, we have earned the trust of clients worldwide.

Client Centric Approach

We take pride in putting our clients first. Our team works closely with shipowners and operators to tailor solutions that meet their specific needs, schedules, and budgets. We believe in building long-lasting partnerships based on trust, reliability, and open communication.

Eco Friendly Approach

We take our responsibility to protect the marine environment seriously. Shams Marine embraces eco-friendly practices and utilizes cutting-edge technologies to ensure that our services have a minimal impact on the ocean and marine life. We comply with all relevant environmental regulations and standards.

Comprehensive Services

Check our Services

Ship Repair

Our ship repair services encompass a comprehensive range of solutions to ensure the optimal functioning and longevity of maritime vessels. From routine maintenance to emergency repairs, our skilled team is equipped to handle a variety of issues, contributing to the safety and efficiency of your fleet.

Cargo Lifting Gears

We specialize in the inspection, maintenance, and repair of cargo lifting gears, including cranes and hoists. Our services ensure that your lifting equipment operates smoothly and adheres to industry standards, promoting a secure and efficient cargo handling process.

Project Cargo Lashing, Securing, and Welding

Safely securing project cargo is paramount to successful transport. Our experts specialize in lashing and securing project cargo to prevent shifting during transit. Additionally, our welding services provide durable and reliable solutions for customized cargo configurations, ensuring the integrity of your shipments.

Cargo Grab Repairing

Efficient cargo handling relies on well-maintained grabs. Our cargo grab repairing services cover a spectrum of issues, from routine maintenance to intricate repairs. We prioritize the swift and precise restoration of your cargo grabs, promoting uninterrupted loading and unloading operations.
